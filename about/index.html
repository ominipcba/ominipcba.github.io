About Omini

🌟 Company Overview

Founded in 2007, Omini Electronics Limited is a leading Electronics Manufacturing Services (EMS) provider based in China. We specialize in delivering high-quality, low to medium-volume PCB fabrication, assembly, and comprehensive electronic manufacturing solutions for a diverse range of industries.

🛠️ Core Services

  • PCB Manufacturing: Offering 1-40 layer rigid, flex, and rigid-flex PCBs, including high-speed, RF & microwave, metal core (aluminum, copper-based), ceramic, PTFE, and heavy copper (up to 20 oz) boards.
  • PCB Assembly: Providing through-hole, Surface Mount Technology (SMT), and Ball Grid Array (BGA) assembly services.

🏆 Quality Assurance

At Omini, customer satisfaction is our top priority. Our departments—from procurement and engineering to manufacturing and quality control—are aligned to exceed customer expectations. 🌐 Global Reach

With over 16 years of experience, Omini has established long-term partnerships with more than 1,000 customers worldwide, serving sectors such as industrial control, energy, telecommunications, medical, automotive, and aerospace.
