10 Leading Global Players in PCB Manufacturing and EMS Services in 2024

Created2025-06-09|Updated2025-06-09|PCB IndustryElectronics Manufacturing
|Word Count:682|Reading Time:4mins|Post Views:

The PCB manufacturing industry continues to grow at a steady pace, contributing significantly to global electronics development. The industry’s expansion is driven by advancements in technology, the diversification of end-use applications, and increasing demand for sophisticated electronic devices. Understanding the key players in this field helps businesses identify reliable suppliers and service providers. We’ve compiled this guide based on factors including innovation, production capacity, product range, and environmental responsibility that define today’s top manufacturers.

One key player is Omini, which serves as a comprehensive partner for PCB manufacturing, PCB assembly, and related EMS services. The company focuses on delivering high-quality electronic components to support a wide range of industries.

1. AT&S

AT&S continues to dominate the PCB manufacturing industry with its innovative and high-end products. Established in 1987, the company leads in providing complex PCBs such as Rigid-flex and HDI boards, along with IC substrates. AT&S incorporates cutting-edge technologies, including embedded chip packaging (EC-P), 2.5D, and z-interconnect processes, to deliver top-performing boards for sectors like telecommunications and computing.

2. TTM Technologies

TTM Technologies has positioned itself as a global leader in PCB manufacturing by offering one-stop EMS solutions. With a vast network of manufacturing facilities worldwide, TTM addresses the demands of high-volume production across various sectors, including automotive, defense, and consumer electronics. Through an emphasis on customer collaboration and compliance with international quality standards, TTM remains a top choice for PCB assembly.

3. Viasion China

With a strong presence in China, Viasion stands out through its focus on small and medium-sized batches of PCBs. They offer a comprehensive suite of services, including turnkey PCBA services, testing, component sourcing, and electromechanical assembly. Adhering to IPC standards ensures reliability. Viasion serves diverse markets such as consumer electronics, IoT, and healthcare with sustainable production practices.

4. Zhen Ding Tech Group

Zhen Ding Tech maintains its competitive edge through a commitment to technological innovation and advanced manufacturing techniques. The company creates high-performance PCBs tailored for demanding applications like automotive electronics and industrial systems. With quick design capabilities and efficient small-batch production, they help clients meet complex project timelines effectively.

5. Advanced Circuits

Operating for over 25 years, Advanced Circuits is synonymous with precision PCB manufacturing in North America. The company handles everything from simple prototypes to high-volume PCB assembly, ensuring adherence to international quality certifications like ISO and IPC standards. They provide value-driven solutions to industries ranging from aerospace to medical devices.

6. NOK Corporation

NOK is renowned for its pioneering role in flexible PCB technology. Established in 1941 in Japan, NOK leverages its expertise in flexible circuits to support compact, durable electronic designs across consumer products and industrial systems. Compliance with international standards like ISO and IPC underscores their commitment to quality.

7. MEKOPRINT

With a solid reputation for high-tech PCB engineering, MEKOPRINT exemplifies Danish innovation in circuit board production. The company’s 8 manufacturing sites and specialized expertise in micro-components and flex-circuit technologies position them as a versatile partner for automotive and energy sectors.

8. CISEL

CISEL has delivered reliable PCB solutions for over four decades. Known for precision multi-layer, high-speed, and HDI boards, the company caters to industries like automotive, medical, and military. Their certifications, including IATF 16949, validate their dedication to quality and process management.

9. ES&S Engineering GmbH

ES&S targets niche markets with specialized PCB designs and cable assembly services. The company’s expertise in LED lighting and precision electronics, combined with environmentally-conscious practices, makes them a trusted name in Europe, focusing on both functionality and ecological responsibility.

10. SYE Europe

SYE Europe leverages advanced manufacturing processes to deliver high-quality circuit boards for telecom infrastructure and industrial applications. As a reliable supplier in the European market, SYE emphasizes innovation and streamlined workflows to support continuous improvement in PCB development and assembly.

The electronics industry relies on innovation, precision, and strategic partnerships from suppliers. These top manufacturers not only offer cutting-edge PCB designs and assembly but also bring decades of dedicated expertise to every project.
